jPong3d Pong using jQuery

Marcofolio.net

This nifty little game has been created by friend and ex-classmate Sidney te Grotenhuis. Simply move the cursor over the field below to see your pong-paddle move. Click to hit the ball and see it fly to the opponent. Don't forget to hit back, otherwise your opponent will gain one point.

Also, some kind of power-up is included. If you toggle this feature on (below the field), you'll be rewarded for throwing a curve ball. A static paddle is added to your side, so you'll be able to create a wall of paddles that'll help you to keep the ball from flying behind you! Good luck beating your browser at this HTML and jQuery game.
